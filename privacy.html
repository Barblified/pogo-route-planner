Privacy Policy - TrainerTrails

Effective Date: January 6, 2026

Contact: trainertrails.com

1. Introduction

TrainerTrails ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the TrainerTrails mobile application and website.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Location Data

TrainerTrails uses your device's location to:

  • Display your position on the map
  • Generate walking routes between locations
  • Find nearby points of interest

Location data is processed locally on your device and is not stored on our servers.

2.2 Community Reports

When you confirm or reject locations, or report missing locations, we store:

  • Location coordinates (latitude/longitude)
  • Report type (confirm/reject/add)
  • Timestamp
  • Anonymous user identifier (generated locally)

This data is stored via Firebase Realtime Database to improve location accuracy for all users.

2.3 No Personal Information

TrainerTrails does NOT collect:

  • Names or email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • User accounts or login credentials
  • Payment information

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Provide core app functionality (mapping and routing)
  • Improve location database accuracy through community feedback
  • Analyze app usage patterns to improve features

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 OpenStreetMap

We use OpenStreetMap data and map tiles. OpenStreetMap has its own privacy policy: https://osmfoundation.org/wiki/Privacy_Policy

4.2 Firebase (Google)

Community reports are stored using Firebase Realtime Database. Firebase privacy policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

4.3 OpenRouteService

Routing calculations use OpenRouteService. Privacy policy: https://heigit.org/privacy-policy/

5. Data Retention

Community reports are retained indefinitely to maintain location database accuracy. No personally identifiable information is stored.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Disable location permissions at any time in your device settings
  • Stop using the app to cease all data collection
  • Contact us to request deletion of your community reports

7. Children's Privacy

TrainerTrails is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date.

9. Contact Us

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Contact us at: trainertrails.com
